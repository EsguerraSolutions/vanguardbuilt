About Us

Founded by Engr. Jonathan Esguerra in 2018, we are an organization that specializes on structural engineering. Our goal is to guarantee the structural integrity of the projects of our clients.

Engr. Jonathan Esguerra is a licensed Civil Engineer and licensed Master Plumber with an extensive experience of 5 years in structural engineering. He passed the Civil Engineering Licensure Exam in 2017 with a rating of 94.05%. He started his structural engineering career in CS Cayanan Civil & Structural Consultancy under the mentorship of ASEP Member and former PICE Angeles City Chapter President Engr. Carlito Cayanan. He also worked as a Project Engineer in Greserr Contractors & Builders Inc. He is knowledgeable with the actual construction practices and always consider them when providing his structural engineering services.
